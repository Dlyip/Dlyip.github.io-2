Queer and Black Pride: Artist Selection

Introduction

This exhibit is held at the City College of San Francisco virtually. It is held on this Github website, which is interactive can be explored by clicking links. Its theme is the intersection of Blackness with LGBTQ2IA+ identities. Through this exhibit, you will learn about Black artists, photographers, musicians, and poets. Every day, Black people are dehumanized and silenced through anti-Black oppression. This theme is important because it showcases the talent of Black queer people and allows us to remember them as empowered humans.
